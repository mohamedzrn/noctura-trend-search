## The Problem with Social Media Trend Intelligence

A trend on TikTok peaks and dies within 48–72 hours. An audio track goes viral, then gets overused and becomes noise within days. **The creators and brands that catch a trend at the right moment can 10x their reach — those who miss it spend money on content no one sees.**

The tools teams currently use — Sprout Social, Later, Hootsuite, Brandwatch — are reporting tools. They tell you what performed last week. They do not tell you what is rising right now, which creators are driving it, or which specific audio or keyword is a genuine signal versus temporary noise.

There is also a fragmentation problem: trends emerge differently on each platform. An audio track breaks on TikTok. A topic erupts on Reddit. A format spreads on Instagram Reels. The same cultural moment expresses itself across all three simultaneously — but no single tool watches all of them, correlates the signals, and tells you "this is the same trend." Brands and creators are still doing this manually, scrolling and guessing, typically learning about a trend two to three days after it peaks.

The market has dozens of analytics tools and zero real-time trend intelligence platforms. That is the gap Noctura fills.

## How It Works

A team member sends a reel to the Noctura bot via Instagram DM. That is the only step they take.

From there, Noctura handles everything automatically. The bot extracts the reel's full metadata — caption, audio track, hashtags, engagement metrics, creator identity, and visual content via AI thumbnail analysis. Claude then analyzes every piece of that data through a structured intelligence prompt, identifying the primary niche, sub-niche, content style, audio virality score on a scale of one to ten, hook strength, shareability indicators, and five to ten niche-defining keywords. When an audio track scores eight or above, the system immediately flags it as a potential breakout and alerts the submitting team member.

Every new analysis updates a rolling niche profile for that creator, built from their last fifty analyzed pieces of content. That profile tracks momentum over time — which niches are rising, stable, or falling — and Claude writes a personalized strategy summary recommending the top two to three content angles to pursue. **The full pipeline, from a forwarded reel to a structured trend analysis and updated creator profile, runs in under 90 seconds.**

All audio tracks and keywords feed into a global data bank shared across every creator in the system. When the same audio appears across multiple unrelated creators, Noctura surfaces it as a cross-creator breakout signal. A real-time web dashboard gives instant access to the trend feed, creator profiles, audio rankings, and keyword trends. Everything syncs to Notion so agency teams and clients can collaborate without logging into a new tool.
